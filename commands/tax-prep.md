# /rei:tax-prep

**Skills:** portfolio-tracker

**Workflow:**
1. Load all properties and monthly actuals for the year
2. Generate Schedule E income and expense summary
3. Calculate depreciation for each property
4. Flag cost segregation candidates
5. Identify 1031 exchange opportunities
6. Generate CPA-ready worksheet

**Output:** Tax preparation worksheet with all Schedule E data.
