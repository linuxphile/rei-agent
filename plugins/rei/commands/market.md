# /rei:market

**Skills:** market-research

**Workflow:**
1. Identify metro and submarket from input
2. Pull demographic data
3. Pull employment data
4. Assess regulatory environment
5. Analyze supply/demand
6. Score submarket
7. Identify top submarkets for investor's strategy
8. Save report to `~/.rei-agent/market_data/reports/`

**Output:** Full market research report.
